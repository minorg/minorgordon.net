Minor Gordon

Professional

I am a software and solutions engineer, technical lead, thinker-doer, and occasional flower arranger.

Since earning my Ph.D. in Computer Science I've worked in industrial research, small businesses and startups, and academia. I thrive in situations that require lateral thinking, wearing multiple hats, and dealing with ambiguity while still creating value.

In my spare time I like to learn new technologies and technical skills and use them to improve access to our cultural heritage online.

Personal

"Minor" is a family name. My father, Dr. Minor W. Gordon, was a clinical psychologist in Claremore, Oklahoma, where I grew up.

My grandfather, Dr. Minor E. Gordon, was a medical doctor in Claremore, as was his father, Dr. Minor H. Gordon.


My wife Jennifer Farley Gordon is a fashion historian. In my free time I work with Jennifer and our colleague Arden Kirkland on projects supporting digital costume collections.


I am passionate about the history of science and technology.

I am a lifelong learner and avid reader, usually of history books.
