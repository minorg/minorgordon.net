Minor Gordon

Professional

Personal

The name "Minor"

"Minor" is a family name. My father, Dr. Minor W. Gordon, was a clinical psychologist in Claremore, Oklahoma, where I grew up.

My grandfather, Dr. Minor E. Gordon, was a medical doctor in Claremore, as was his father, Dr. Minor H. Gordon.
